# Default Voice Template

## Tone
Professional yet approachable. Clear and direct.

## Structure
- Lead with the most important insight
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Include data points to support claims
- End with a forward-looking statement or call to action

## Style
- Active voice preferred
- Avoid jargon unless the audience expects it
- Use specific numbers over vague qualifiers
- One idea per paragraph

## Formatting
- **Bold** for key terms on first mention
- Use bullet lists for 3+ items
- Include section headers for articles over 500 words
